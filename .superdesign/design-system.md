# SkillChain AI Current Design System

## Brand

SkillChain AI is a proof-of-skill network combining explainable AI, public developer evidence, Stellar credentials, and borderless professional payments.

## Current Palette

- Obsidian forest: `#07140f`
- Deep surface: `#0c1c15`
- Raised surface: `#12261c`
- Acid mint: `#7cf39d`
- Electric lime: `#b7ff7a`
- Editorial cream: `#f4f2e9`
- Muted text: `#9bb0a2`

## Typography

- Interface: Manrope
- Display: Space Grotesk
- Editorial accent: Instrument Serif
- Data and labels: DM Mono

## Geometry

- Current controls and cards are mostly square
- Borders are one pixel and low contrast
- Primary actions use electric lime
- Dashboard uses a fixed sidebar and fluid content canvas

## Motion

- Short 160–250ms transitions
- Floating credential chips
- Soft glow blooms
- Spinner and modal entrance animations

## Accessibility

- Maintain WCAG AA text contrast
- Preserve keyboard focus visibility
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Keep hit areas at least 44 pixels
